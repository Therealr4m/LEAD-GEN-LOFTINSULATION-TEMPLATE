How effective is loft insulation?

Loft insulation helps slow the rate of heat escaping through the roof, which can improve how efficiently your home retains warmth during colder months.

Will loft insulation affect roof ventilation?

Correct installation should maintain ventilation pathways around the eaves so air can still move through the roof structure.

Is loft insulation suitable for all roof types?

Most roof structures can accommodate some form of loft insulation. The exact approach may vary depending on the loft design and available space.

What materials are used for loft insulation?

Mineral wool insulation is commonly used for standard loft floors due to its thermal performance and ease of installation.

How do installers ensure even insulation coverage?

Installers distribute insulation across the loft floor methodically to achieve consistent depth and avoid gaps that reduce effectiveness.
