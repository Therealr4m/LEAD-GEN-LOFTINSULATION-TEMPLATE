What depth should loft insulation be installed to?

Modern guidance usually recommends around 270mm of mineral wool insulation across the loft floor. Many homes currently have less than this, which means increasing the depth can improve thermal performance.

Does loft insulation reduce heating bills?

By slowing the rate at which heat escapes through the roof, loft insulation helps your heating system retain warmth more efficiently. This can help reduce the amount of energy required to keep your home comfortable.

Will loft insulation affect my loft storage?

Insulation is typically installed across the loft floor, which can raise the floor level slightly depending on the depth installed. Raised loft boarding can sometimes be used if storage space is required above the insulation.

Is loft insulation messy to install?

Professional installation is usually straightforward and contained within the loft space. Installers typically work carefully to ensure insulation is fitted evenly without disturbing the rest of the property.

Do newer homes still need loft insulation?

Even relatively modern homes can benefit from checking their insulation levels. In some cases insulation may not reach recommended depths or may have been disturbed over time.
