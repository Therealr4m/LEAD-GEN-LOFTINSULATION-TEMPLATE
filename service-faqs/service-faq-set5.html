Do all homes need loft insulation?

Most homes benefit from having sufficient loft insulation because it helps reduce heat escaping through the roof. Properties with little or outdated insulation can often improve efficiency significantly.

What happens if loft insulation is installed unevenly?

Uneven insulation coverage can leave areas where heat escapes more easily. Proper installation ensures consistent depth and coverage across the loft floor.

Can loft insulation be upgraded later?

Yes. If insulation levels are currently low, additional insulation can often be installed later to increase the overall depth and improve performance.

Does loft insulation require maintenance?

Loft insulation generally requires very little maintenance, but it can be helpful to occasionally check that it remains evenly distributed and hasn’t been disturbed.

Is professional installation necessary?

Professional installation ensures insulation is fitted evenly, ventilation pathways remain open and recommended depth levels are achieved across the loft.
