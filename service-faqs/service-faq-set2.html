Is loft insulation worth installing?

Loft insulation is one of the most effective ways to reduce heat loss in a property. Because heat rises, a poorly insulated loft can allow warmth to escape quickly. Installing insulation helps improve overall energy efficiency.

Can loft insulation be installed over existing insulation?

In many cases additional insulation can be installed over existing material if it is still in good condition. This is often referred to as a loft insulation top-up and helps bring insulation closer to modern depth standards.

Will loft insulation affect loft ventilation?

Proper installation should maintain ventilation at the eaves so airflow through the roof space is not restricted. Maintaining airflow is important to prevent condensation issues within the loft structure.

What type of loft insulation is usually installed?

Mineral wool insulation is commonly used for standard loft floors because it provides effective thermal performance and can be installed to recommended depths. In some lofts with limited space, rigid PIR boards may also be used.

Do I need to empty my loft before installation?

It’s helpful if the loft floor is reasonably clear so insulation can be installed evenly across the space. If items are present, installers can normally work around them, but clear access makes installation easier and faster.
