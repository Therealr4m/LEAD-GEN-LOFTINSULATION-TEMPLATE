Why is loft insulation important?

Because heat naturally rises, a significant amount of heat can escape through the roof of a poorly insulated home. Loft insulation helps create a barrier that slows this heat loss and improves overall efficiency.

How do I know if my loft insulation needs upgrading?

If insulation appears thin, uneven or below recommended depth levels, it may benefit from being upgraded or topped up. Many older installations fall short of modern standards.

Can loft insulation help with temperature control?

Insulation helps stabilise indoor temperatures by slowing heat movement through the roof. This can help homes stay warmer in winter and slightly cooler during warmer months.

Is loft insulation installed between joists?

Mineral wool insulation is typically installed between and across loft joists to achieve the correct depth. This approach helps create consistent coverage across the loft floor.

How long does loft insulation last?

Loft insulation can last for many years if it remains dry and undisturbed. However, performance can decline if insulation becomes compressed or uneven over time.
