How much loft insulation should my loft have?

Most UK homes should have around 270mm of loft insulation to meet modern recommendations. Many older properties have significantly less, which means heat can escape through the roof more easily. Bringing insulation up to the correct depth improves overall thermal efficiency.

How long does loft insulation installation take?

Most loft insulation installations can be completed within a single day depending on the size and layout of the loft. Larger or more complex loft spaces may take longer, particularly if access is limited or preparation work is required.

Will installing loft insulation make my home warmer?

Loft insulation helps reduce heat escaping through the roof, which allows your home to retain warmth more effectively. While it doesn’t generate heat itself, it helps the heat already produced in the home stay inside for longer.

Do you install loft insulation in older homes?

Yes. Many older properties benefit significantly from loft insulation installation because they often have little or poorly fitted insulation. Installing insulation correctly can improve thermal performance without altering the structure of the home.
