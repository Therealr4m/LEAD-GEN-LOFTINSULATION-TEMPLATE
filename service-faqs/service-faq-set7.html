Why do many homes still have poor loft insulation?

In many properties insulation was installed years ago when recommended depth levels were lower. As standards improved, older installations were often left unchanged.

Can loft insulation help improve home comfort?

By slowing heat loss through the roof, insulation can help maintain a more stable indoor temperature during colder months.

Does loft insulation take up much space?

Insulation sits across the loft floor and its depth depends on the recommended standard being installed. Modern installations often reach around 270mm.

What happens during a loft insulation installation?

Installers assess the loft, prepare the space and then install insulation evenly across the loft floor while maintaining airflow at the edges.

Is it possible to install insulation in a small loft?

Even small loft spaces can usually be insulated effectively as long as installers can safely access the loft and distribute insulation evenly.
