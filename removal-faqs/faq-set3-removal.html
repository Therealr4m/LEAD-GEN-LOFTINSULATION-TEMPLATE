Is it worth removing insulation that still looks okay?

In some cases, yes. Insulation can appear fine on the surface but may be holding moisture or be uneven underneath. A proper assessment helps determine whether it’s still performing as it should.

Can loft insulation removal help with lingering smells?

If odours are coming from damp, mould or pest contamination within the insulation, removing it can eliminate the source of the smell and improve the overall condition of the loft space.

Do I need to leave the property during the removal process?

In most cases, no. The work is carried out within the loft space, and disruption is kept minimal, so homeowners can usually remain in the property while the removal is completed.

What condition will my loft be left in after removal?

The loft will be cleared of insulation and debris, leaving a clean surface that’s ready for inspection, ventilation improvements or new insulation to be installed properly.

Can pests in insulation affect whether it needs removing?

Yes, pest activity often contaminates insulation and affects its performance. In these situations, removal is usually recommended to fully clear the affected material before moving forward.
