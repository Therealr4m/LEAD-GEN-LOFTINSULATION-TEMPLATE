What should I do before booking loft insulation removal?

The main thing is understanding the condition of your current insulation. If you’ve noticed damp, odours, uneven material or signs of pests, it’s worth having the loft assessed properly so you know whether full or partial removal is needed.

Is loft insulation removal needed if my insulation is just old?

Not always, but age can affect performance. Older insulation is more likely to be compressed, uneven or affected by past conditions. If it’s no longer performing properly, removal may be the better option before upgrading.

Can removing insulation help prepare the loft for future work?

Yes, clearing the loft creates a clean and accessible space, making it easier to carry out any future work such as ventilation improvements, boarding or installing new insulation correctly.

How do you ensure all insulation is fully removed?

We work across the entire loft area methodically, including edges, corners and around obstacles. The aim is to remove all affected material so nothing is left behind that could impact future performance.

What’s the risk of leaving damaged insulation in place?

Leaving insulation that’s damp, contaminated or degraded can allow problems like moisture retention or poor airflow to continue. Over time, this can affect the condition of the loft and reduce the effectiveness of any future insulation improvements.
