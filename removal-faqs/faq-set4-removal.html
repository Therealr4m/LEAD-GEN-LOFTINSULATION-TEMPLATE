Is loft insulation removal necessary before upgrading insulation?

Not always, but if the existing insulation is damp, uneven or contaminated, removing it first ensures any new insulation performs properly without underlying issues.

How do you deal with insulation around wiring or pipes?

We carefully work around cables, pipework and other fixtures to remove insulation safely without disturbing important elements within the loft.

Can old insulation affect the performance of new insulation?

Yes, if old insulation is left in poor condition underneath, it can reduce airflow or trap moisture, which affects how well new insulation performs over time.

Will removing insulation expose any hidden problems?

It often does. Once insulation is cleared, issues like damp areas, airflow blockages or structural concerns become easier to identify and address properly.

What’s the main benefit of removing old insulation properly?

It gives you a clean starting point. Instead of layering over potential issues, the loft can be assessed and improved properly, leading to better long-term performance.
