What happens to the old insulation once it’s removed?

Removed insulation is carefully bagged and taken away for appropriate disposal. We ensure the loft space is cleared properly so no loose material or debris is left behind.

Do you remove all insulation or just the damaged areas?

That depends on the condition of the loft. In some cases, only certain sections need removing, but where insulation is widely affected by damp, contamination or poor installation, full removal is usually the better option.

Will there be dust inside my home during removal?

We use controlled removal methods and protect the loft hatch area to minimise dust entering the home. While some disturbance is unavoidable, it’s kept as low as possible with the right equipment and approach.

Can insulation be removed if my loft is difficult to access?

Yes, most lofts can still be worked in even with limited access. The method may vary depending on hatch size and layout, but removal can usually be carried out safely with the right preparation.

Do you check for underlying issues during removal?

Yes, removal gives a clear view of the loft space, allowing us to identify problems such as ventilation issues, signs of moisture or previous damage before any new insulation is installed.
