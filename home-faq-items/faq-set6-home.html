What happens if my loft insulation is uneven?

Uneven insulation can lead to inconsistent heat retention. Adjusting or topping up the insulation helps create a more even thermal layer across the loft.

Do newer homes still need loft insulation upgrades?

Some newer properties may still benefit from improved insulation, especially if installation was minimal or not carried out to full recommended depth.

Can insulation be installed around pipes and wiring?

Yes, insulation can be carefully fitted around existing services while maintaining safe spacing and airflow where needed.

Will insulation help keep my home cooler in summer?

Yes, insulation slows down heat transfer in both directions, helping to keep heat out during warmer months and retain warmth in winter.

How do I know if my insulation needs upgrading?

If your insulation is below recommended depth, uneven, or installed many years ago, it may no longer be performing as effectively as it should.
