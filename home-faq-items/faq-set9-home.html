Does loft insulation need to be maintained?

Generally it requires very little maintenance, but it’s worth checking occasionally to ensure it hasn’t been disturbed or compressed.

Can insulation help reduce condensation issues?

When installed correctly with proper ventilation, insulation can help stabilise temperatures and reduce the likelihood of condensation forming.

Will insulation block access to my loft?

No, access points are kept clear. Installations are planned to ensure safe and practical access remains.

Is there a best time of year to install insulation?

Insulation can be installed at any time of year, though many homeowners choose to upgrade before colder months.

Can insulation be installed over existing boards?

This depends on the setup. In some cases, boards may need adjusting to allow insulation to reach the correct depth.
