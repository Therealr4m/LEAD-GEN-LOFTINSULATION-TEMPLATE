How long does loft insulation last once installed?

Loft insulation is designed to last for many years when installed properly. However, its effectiveness can reduce over time if it becomes compressed, disturbed or affected by moisture.

Do I need to prepare anything before installation?

In most cases, only basic access to the loft is needed. If the loft is heavily boarded or used for storage, some preparation may be required to allow access to the insulation area.

Will loft insulation help with draughts in my home?

Loft insulation helps reduce heat escaping through the roof, which can improve overall warmth. While it doesn’t directly seal draughts, it contributes to a more consistent indoor temperature.

How quickly will I notice a difference after installation?

Many homeowners notice improved warmth fairly quickly, especially during colder periods. The full benefit is usually seen as the property retains heat more effectively over time.
