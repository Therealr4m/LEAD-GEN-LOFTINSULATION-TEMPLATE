How important is insulation depth?

Depth plays a key role in performance. Insufficient depth reduces effectiveness, which is why modern standards recommend around 270mm.

Can I install loft insulation myself?

While possible, professional installation ensures correct coverage, depth and ventilation, which all affect long-term performance.

Will insulation reduce outside noise?

Insulation can help slightly with sound reduction, though its primary purpose is improving thermal performance.

Do all lofts have the same insulation requirements?

No, each loft is different. The correct approach depends on structure, layout and existing insulation levels.

Is insulation still effective if it’s been there for years?

Older insulation may still provide some benefit, but it’s often below current standards and may not perform as efficiently as newer installations.
