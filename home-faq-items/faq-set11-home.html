What’s the main purpose of loft insulation?

The main goal is to reduce heat escaping through the roof, improving overall energy efficiency and indoor comfort.

Does insulation work immediately after installation?

Yes, once installed correctly, it begins reducing heat loss straight away, with noticeable improvements over time.

Will insulation affect my loft hatch area?

The loft hatch is usually insulated as part of the process to prevent heat escaping through that point.

Is loft insulation suitable for all roof types?

Most pitched roofs are suitable, though the approach may vary depending on structure and access.

Can insulation improve overall home comfort?

Yes, by reducing heat loss, insulation helps maintain a more stable and comfortable indoor temperature.
