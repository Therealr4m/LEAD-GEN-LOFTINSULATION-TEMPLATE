What areas of the home benefit most from loft insulation?

The biggest impact is reducing heat loss through the roof, which improves the overall warmth and efficiency of the entire property.

Does insulation need to cover the entire loft?

For best performance, insulation should be evenly distributed across the entire loft area without gaps or compressed sections.

Can loft insulation improve EPC ratings?

Yes, improving insulation levels can contribute to better EPC performance by reducing overall heat loss from the property.

What if my loft has been converted?

Converted lofts require a different insulation approach, often involving insulation within the roof structure rather than at ceiling level.

Is loft insulation suitable for bungalows?

Yes, bungalows often benefit significantly from insulation as a large proportion of heat loss can occur through the roof space.
