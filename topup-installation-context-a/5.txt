Properties across {{LOCATION}} often contain a mixture of insulation conditions depending on the age of the house and any previous upgrades. Some lofts have thin historic layers, while others contain insulation that has settled unevenly over time.

<br> During a top-up installation, additional insulation is installed carefully across the loft floor to increase depth evenly. By layering insulation between and across the joists, the loft gains improved thermal coverage without compressing the existing material beneath.
