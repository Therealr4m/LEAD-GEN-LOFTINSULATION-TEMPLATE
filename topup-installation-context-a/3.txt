No two loft spaces in {{LOCATION}} are identical. Structural differences in roof framing, joist height and previous insulation work all influence how a loft insulation top-up should be installed.
<br>For this reason, insulation is installed methodically across the loft floor, building depth evenly between and over joists rather than stacking material in isolated areas. This controlled installation approach helps maintain airflow while ensuring the insulation performs consistently across the entire roof space.
