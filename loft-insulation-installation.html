Here Are Our Other Loft Insulation Services

Loft Insulation Removal

Safe removal of damaged, contaminated or ineffective insulation before proper reinstallation.

Rigid PIR Insulation

High-performance insulation boards for lofts with limited headroom or conversion requirements.

Loft Insulation Top-Ups

Upgrade existing insulation that falls below recommended depth without full replacement.
  • Full loft insulation installation to 270mm recommended depth
  • Mineral wool insulation systems
  • Knauf Earthwool and Rockwool materials
  • Rigid PIR board installation where headroom is limited
  • Careful installation around eaves, joists and loft hatches

We focus on correct fitting and long-term performance rather than quick surface-level solutions that might do their job short term, but into the future problems can occur.
