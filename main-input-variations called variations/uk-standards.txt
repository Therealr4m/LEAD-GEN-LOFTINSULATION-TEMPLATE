
Up to 25% of a home's heat can escape through the roof if loft insulation is below modern standards.

A poorly insulated loft is one of the most common sources of heat loss in UK homes.

Improving loft insulation is one of the most effective ways to improve overall home energy efficiency.

When insulation depth is too low, heat rises through the ceiling and escapes through the roof space.

Correct insulation depth helps slow down the transfer of heat through the roof structure.
