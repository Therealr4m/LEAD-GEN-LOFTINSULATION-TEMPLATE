What happens if my loft has been boarded over?

If insulation sits below boarding and can’t be accessed properly, sections may need to be lifted to assess what’s underneath. In some cases, raised boarding solutions can be recommended to allow proper insulation depth.

Is it worth upgrading loft insulation if I already have some installed?

In many cases, yes. A lot of properties still have insulation well below the recommended depth, and topping it up can significantly improve heat retention and overall comfort.

Do you install insulation differently depending on the type of property?

Yes, the approach varies depending on roof structure, loft access and available space. Tighter lofts or converted areas may require different materials or installation methods.

How do you deal with hard-to-reach areas in the loft?

We ensure insulation is installed into all accessible areas, including tight corners and around structural elements, as these are often where heat loss occurs if left uncovered.

Will the installation improve how my home feels day-to-day?

Yes, once installed properly, most homeowners notice more consistent room temperatures and improved heat retention, especially during colder months.
