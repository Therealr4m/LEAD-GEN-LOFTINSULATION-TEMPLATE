Do you remove old insulation if it’s contaminated or damaged?

Yes, if insulation shows signs of damp, mould, pest activity or heavy compression, removing it is usually the best option before installing new material to ensure performance isn’t affected.

How do you handle loft insulation around pipes and wiring?

We carefully work around pipes and cables, making sure insulation is fitted without causing damage or overheating risks, while still maintaining consistent coverage.

Will insulation affect my loft storage space?

It can reduce visible floor space if installed to full depth, but solutions like raised boarding can be used to maintain storage while still achieving proper insulation levels.

Can insulation be installed if my loft has limited headroom?

Yes, in tighter lofts we may use alternative materials like rigid PIR boards where appropriate, allowing insulation to perform well without taking up excessive space

Do you assess the condition of the loft before quoting?

Yes, a proper quote is based on assessing your current insulation, loft layout and any issues present, rather than giving a generic price without understanding the space.
