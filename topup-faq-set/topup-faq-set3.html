What happens if my loft insulation is uneven?

Uneven insulation is common in older lofts. During a loft insulation top-up the existing material is usually levelled before new insulation is installed.

Can I install a loft insulation top-up myself?

While it is possible, many homeowners prefer professional installation to ensure insulation is installed evenly and ventilation remains clear.

Will topping up insulation improve energy efficiency?

Increasing insulation depth reduces the rate at which heat escapes through the roof, which helps the property retain warmth more efficiently.

Does insulation settle over time?

Some insulation materials can compress or shift slightly over the years, which is one reason loft insulation top-ups are often needed.

Is loft insulation installed between or over the joists?

During a top-up, insulation is usually installed both between and across ceiling joists to build consistent depth.
