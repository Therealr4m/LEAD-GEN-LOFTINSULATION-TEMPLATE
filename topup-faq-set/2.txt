<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Is a loft insulation top-up cheaper than full replacement?</h3>
<p>In most cases it is. If the existing insulation is still in good condition, adding additional insulation is usually far more cost-effective than removing everything and reinstalling from scratch.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>How long does a loft insulation top-up take?</h3>
<p>Most loft insulation top-ups can be completed within a single day depending on the size of the loft and how accessible the space is.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Can loft insulation be topped up more than once?</h3>
<p>Yes. As long as ventilation remains clear and insulation stays in good condition, additional depth can be added if required.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Will topping up insulation affect loft ventilation?</h3>
<p>Proper installation ensures airflow paths around the eaves remain clear so the loft continues to ventilate naturally.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Does a loft insulation top-up create mess inside the home?</h3>
<p>Most work takes place inside the loft space itself, so disruption to the rest of the property is usually minimal.</p>
</div>
