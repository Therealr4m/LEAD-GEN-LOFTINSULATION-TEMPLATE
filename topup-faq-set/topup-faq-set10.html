Can loft insulation reduce cold spots in the home?

By reducing heat escaping through the roof, insulation improvements can help create a more balanced indoor temperature.

Is loft insulation installed above the ceiling?

Yes, insulation is typically installed across the loft floor rather than inside the roof rafters.

Does insulation affect summer temperatures?

Insulation primarily reduces winter heat loss but can also help slow heat entering the home during warmer periods.

Can insulation be added around roof timbers?

Yes, insulation is positioned carefully between and across structural timbers during installation.

Is loft insulation considered an energy efficiency upgrade?

Yes, improving loft insulation depth is one of the most common ways to improve home energy efficiency.
