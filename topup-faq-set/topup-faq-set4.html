Can loft insulation help reduce heating costs?

By slowing the rate at which heat escapes through the roof, improved insulation depth can help homes retain warmth for longer.

Will my loft still be usable after insulation is topped up?

Yes, although adjustments may be needed if loft boards or stored items currently restrict insulation depth.

Does insulation need to be removed before topping up?

Not usually. If the existing insulation is dry and stable, new insulation can simply be installed on top.

How much heat can escape through the roof?

In poorly insulated homes, around a quarter of total heat loss can occur through the roof.

Can loft insulation help keep rooms warmer?

Increasing insulation depth helps stabilise indoor temperatures by slowing heat loss through the roof structure.
