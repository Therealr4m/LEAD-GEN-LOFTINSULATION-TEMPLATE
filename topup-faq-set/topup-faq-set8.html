What happens if insulation blocks airflow?

Correct installation ensures ventilation paths remain clear so moisture cannot build up in the roof space.

Is insulation installed across the whole loft?

Yes, insulation is typically installed across the entire loft floor to maintain consistent thermal coverage.

Does insulation work instantly?

Once installed, insulation immediately begins slowing the rate at which heat escapes through the roof.

Can loft insulation help with winter heating?

Increasing insulation depth helps the property retain warmth for longer after heating is used.

Do loft insulation top-ups require structural changes?

In most cases they do not, although minor adjustments may be needed if boards or storage limit insulation depth.
