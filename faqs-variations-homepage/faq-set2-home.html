Can loft insulation help reduce energy bills?

By reducing heat loss through the roof, loft insulation helps your home retain warmth more efficiently. This can reduce how often heating is needed, especially during colder months.

Will loft insulation affect ventilation in my roof?

Properly installed insulation should not block airflow. Maintaining ventilation is an important part of the installation process to prevent condensation and ensure long-term performance.

How do I know if my loft insulation needs upgrading?

If your insulation is below 270mm, uneven, or installed many years ago, it’s likely not performing as well as it should. Many homeowners only realise this after noticing heat escaping or higher heating usage.

How disruptive is loft insulation installation?

Most loft insulation work is carried out within the loft space itself, so disruption inside the home is usually minimal. Access is typically through the loft hatch, and the main living areas are not affected during installation.
