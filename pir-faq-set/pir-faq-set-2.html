Why do some lofts use rigid PIR insulation instead of mineral wool?

Rigid PIR insulation provides much higher thermal performance per millimetre of thickness, making it useful in roof structures where insulation depth is limited.

Can PIR insulation improve home energy efficiency?

Yes. By reducing heat transfer through the roof structure, PIR insulation can help improve thermal efficiency and reduce heat loss within the property.

Do PIR insulation boards need to be sealed?

In many installations foil tape is used to seal joints between boards. This helps maintain the vapour control layer and improves insulation continuity.

Is PIR insulation suitable for small loft spaces?

Because PIR boards deliver strong insulation performance in a thinner profile, they are often chosen for lofts where available insulation space is limited.

Is PIR insulation more expensive than other loft insulation?

PIR boards generally cost more than mineral wool insulation, largely due to material cost and the precision required during installation.
