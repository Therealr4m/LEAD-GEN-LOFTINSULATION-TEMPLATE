Why is PIR insulation considered high-performance?

PIR boards offer strong thermal resistance compared to many traditional insulation materials, meaning less thickness is required to achieve effective insulation.

Where are PIR boards typically installed in lofts?

They are often fitted between rafters or above joists depending on the insulation design and the intended use of the loft space.

Do PIR boards need airflow above them?

Most roof insulation systems maintain ventilation space above the insulation layer to prevent condensation.

Can PIR insulation help reduce heating demand?

Improving roof insulation reduces heat loss from the property, which can lower the amount of energy needed to maintain indoor temperatures.

Do PIR boards require maintenance?

Once installed correctly, PIR insulation generally requires very little maintenance and continues to perform for many years.
