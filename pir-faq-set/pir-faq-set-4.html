Is rigid PIR insulation suitable for roof rafters?

Yes. PIR boards are commonly installed between roof rafters as part of warm roof insulation systems and loft conversions.

Does PIR insulation help maintain indoor temperatures?

By reducing heat transfer through the roof, PIR insulation can help stabilise indoor temperatures throughout the year.

Can PIR insulation contribute to condensation problems?

Condensation usually results from poor ventilation rather than the insulation itself. Proper airflow design prevents moisture build-up.

Do PIR boards need professional installation?

Because PIR insulation must be cut accurately and fitted tightly, professional installation is often recommended.

How long does PIR insulation installation take?

Installation time depends on loft size and complexity, but PIR boards generally require more careful fitting than loose insulation rolls.
