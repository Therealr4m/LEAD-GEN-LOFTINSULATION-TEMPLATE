What thermal value do PIR insulation boards provide?

Rigid PIR boards have a low thermal conductivity rating, meaning they resist heat transfer effectively compared with many traditional insulation materials.

Are PIR boards used in loft conversions?

Yes. Their slim profile and strong insulation performance make them well suited to loft conversion roof structures.

Can PIR insulation improve EPC ratings?

Improving roof insulation can contribute to better overall thermal performance, which may support improved EPC ratings.

Do PIR boards need to be taped at the joints?

Foil tape is often used to seal joints between boards, helping maintain the vapour control layer and improve insulation continuity.

Is PIR insulation suitable for older homes?

Many older properties benefit from PIR insulation where roof space is limited and stronger insulation performance is required.
