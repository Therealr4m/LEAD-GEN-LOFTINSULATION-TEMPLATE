Can PIR insulation be installed during roof upgrades?

Yes. PIR boards are often installed during roof renovations or loft conversions where improved insulation performance is required.

Why is accurate cutting important with PIR boards?

Boards must fit tightly within the roof structure to prevent gaps that allow heat to bypass the insulation layer.

Do PIR boards require vapour control layers?

Many roof insulation systems include vapour control layers alongside PIR boards to manage moisture movement within the roof structure.

Can PIR insulation improve loft comfort?

By reducing heat transfer through the roof structure, PIR insulation helps maintain more stable indoor temperatures.

Is PIR insulation suitable for tight roof spaces?

Because PIR delivers strong insulation performance in a thinner profile, it is often chosen where rafter depth or headroom is limited.
