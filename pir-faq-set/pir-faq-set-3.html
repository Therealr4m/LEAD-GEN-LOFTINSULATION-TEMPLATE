How thick should rigid PIR insulation boards be?

Board thickness depends on the thermal performance required and the depth of the roof structure. Many installations use boards between 50mm and 120mm thick.

Can PIR insulation be installed in an existing loft?

Yes. PIR boards can be installed within existing roof structures if the layout and ventilation design allow for correct installation.

Does PIR insulation require ventilation space?

Most roof insulation systems require airflow above the insulation layer to prevent condensation and protect roof timbers from moisture build-up.

Why is tight fitting important with PIR boards?

Gaps between boards can create thermal bridging, allowing heat to bypass the insulation layer and reducing overall performance.

Can PIR boards be used with other insulation materials?

Some insulation systems combine PIR boards with additional insulation layers to achieve the required thermal performance.
