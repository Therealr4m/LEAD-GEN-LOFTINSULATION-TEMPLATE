What makes rigid PIR insulation different from loft insulation rolls?

Rigid PIR boards provide significantly higher thermal resistance per thickness compared to traditional mineral wool loft rolls. This allows strong insulation performance even where loft depth is limited.

Where is rigid PIR insulation usually installed in a loft?

PIR boards are typically installed between rafters or above joists depending on the roof design and whether the loft is used as a living space.

Do PIR boards require ventilation gaps?

Yes. Most roof insulation systems require airflow space above the insulation layer to prevent condensation and protect roof timbers.

Are PIR boards suitable for loft conversions?

Yes. PIR insulation is commonly used in loft conversions because it delivers high insulation performance within a slim profile.

How long does PIR insulation last?

When installed correctly, rigid PIR boards are designed to deliver long-term insulation performance for many years.
